About Bent and his work

Bent lives in Cottage Grove, Oregon, where he offers custom house design as bj archdesign.

He has owned and operated his business designing and building custom homes in California and Oregon for the last 40 years.

Bent grew up and was trained in Denmark as a skilled craftsman who has worked on some of Copenhagen's oldest and most famous historical and government buildings, including Amalienborg Palace, the Royal Palace in Copenhagen.

My work reflects my Danish training in building design and construction.
